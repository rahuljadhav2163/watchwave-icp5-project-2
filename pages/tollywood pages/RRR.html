RRR

SYNOPSIS

RRR is an entirely fictitious story incorporating the lives of two real-life Indian revolutionaries, namely Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem, who fought against the British Raj and the Nizam of Hyderabad.Charan plays Rama Raju while Rama Rao plays Komaram Bheem.A fictitious story about two legendary revolutionaries and their journey away from home before they started fighting for their country in the 1920s.
